Candidates Debates Letter from David Rosenberg Platform PHD on Facebook

The PHD Party thanks you for you support. A Message from David:

From the bottom of my heart, I would like to thank all of you who made this possible. For the last eight months, it has been an honor and a privilege to serve you as the Assembly Speaker, and I promise you that I will bring the same passion, energy, and devotion to the presidency from this moment on, until the day I graduate from Brooklyn College.

I want to congratulate all the newly-elected members of our government for their achievements, and thank them for their work these last few days. I look forward to working closely with each of you, and plan to assist in the transition in every way I can.

I cannot fully express my appreciation for the people who made all this possible-especially those who did not have a position to run for. The names of all these people would be too much to write out, but I would like to give a shout-out to Joshua Hoffman, Charlie Kerr, Tim Andrew Donnelly, and Abraham Haimed. I would be writing a very different statement without you guys.

This list would be incomplete without mentioning the USL Floor Leader Kenji Logie. Over the last eight months, the partnership between two formerly bitter rivals has ushered in a new era of achievement and progress. And our success these last few months would've been impossible without Kenji's hard work.

I am not sure how I would've been able to do any of this without my trusted deputy and dear friend, Vice President-elect Ayala Lieberman. Ayala, you have no idea how much you have done for me, and how much our friendship means to me. I cannot imagine having done this with anyone else, and for that I can only say thank you.

But more important than anyone else, I want to thank Jamell Henderson for running a clean campaign, putting a noble fight, and making sure that I stayed on the issues, and making sure that I was doing everything I had to for the students of Brooklyn College. Jamell, though the last few days have been intense, I really do consider you a friend and a partner, and I look forward to working with you over the next year.
